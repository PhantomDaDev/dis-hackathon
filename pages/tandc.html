Spirit of the Hackathon

Remember that hackathons are like marathons. Some people go to compete but most people take part to better themselves and have fun. Whatever the reason is, you're at a hackathon, make sure you're upholding the hacker spirit by collaborating with other teams and having fun.

Rules of the Hackathon

  1. All work on a project should be done at the hackathon.
  2. The use of pre-existing code or projects is not allowed.
  3. Use of AI tools such as ChatGPT, Gemini, etc. is strictly prohibited.
  4. Any form of cheating, plagiarism, or unfair practices will result in immediate disqualification.
  5. Teams must stop hacking once the time is up.
